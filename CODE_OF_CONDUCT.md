# Code of Conduct

AI Language Partner welcomes contributors who want to improve local-first
language learning software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but correct harmful behavior quickly.
- Keep reviews about the code, docs, tests, and learner experience.
- Credit language, voice, and content contributions accurately.
- Be careful with beginner learners and language communities; avoid stereotypes
  or shaming.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory remarks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Spam, low-effort PR farming, or artificial contribution inflation.
- Adding generated content without review provenance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or PRs; or block
participants who make the project unsafe or unproductive.
